About

I am a senior Machine Learning Researcher and Research Coordinator at Deezer, a global music streaming service. With over nine years of experience, I specialize in large-scale recommendation systems, focusing on deep reinforcement learning, sequential modeling, multi-modal representations, and recently, large language models (LLMs) applied to music recommendation to improve personalization and user engagement.

Prior to being a researcher, I spent five years as a Machine Learning Engineer building a state of the art music recommender system at Deezer.
