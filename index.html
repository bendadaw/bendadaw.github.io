About

I’m a machine learning researcher with 9 years of experience in building and deploying large-scale music recommendation systems. My work focuses on sequential models, generative AI, and multi-modal machine learning with real-world applications in personalization and exploration.

I currently serve as Research Coordinator at Deezer and am completing a PhD at Paris Dauphine University, focusing on sequential learning in music recommendation.
